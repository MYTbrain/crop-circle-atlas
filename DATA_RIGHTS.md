# Data and image rights

The MIT license covers the software in this repository. It does not relicense
third-party source material.

- Formation metadata is stored with its source URL and retrieval record.
- Source photographs and diagrams remain the property of their respective
  photographers, researchers, archives, and publishers. The public dataset
  links to source pages; it does not redistribute their images.
- `COMBINED.pdf` is a user-supplied local source and is not copied into this
  repository. Its SHA-256 digest is recorded for reproducibility.
- GeoNames place data is licensed under CC BY 4.0 and is used only to attach
  approximate locality centroids. See <https://www.geonames.org/about.html>.
- OpenStreetMap map data is copyright OpenStreetMap contributors and available
  under ODbL. Esri World Imagery is displayed with its required attribution.

Before publishing a georeferenced aerial photograph, obtain permission from
the rights holder and record that permission in `data/image_assets.csv`.
