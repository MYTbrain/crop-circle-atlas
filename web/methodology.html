Reports and coordinates

Each source statement is retained separately before conservative entity merging. Dates retain their precision. Most map points are approximate locality centroids, not exact fields; every coordinate records its method and uncertainty.

The current build contains 8,390 assertions and 7,749 conservative catalog entities. The bounded source expansion preserves source geography and leaves 167 alias and 83 probable-overlap candidates unmerged; 450 new normalized keys are not claimed as 450 proven-new formations.

Straight components and orientation

  1. A straight-component candidate is an image-space detection.
  2. A diagram angle is measured in an image whose north direction may be unknown.
  3. A true azimuth is a geographic bearing clockwise from true north.
  4. Only a reviewed true azimuth with evidence and uncertainty can create an atlas projection.

The registration lab uses four to twelve paired landmarks to correct rotation, skew, and perspective locally in your browser. Two clicks along a component then produce a traceable true-north bearing, uncertainty, and export package.

The evidence qualifies the local orientation, not its extension across hundreds of kilometres. Every long-distance line is labeled experimental and has no demonstrated predictive validity.

Alignment tests

Corridor hits use spherical cross-track and along-track distances. Statistical eligibility also requires origin, target-coordinate, and distance-dependent angular uncertainty to fit inside the corridor. All current results remain exploratory until the range, corridor, directionality, date ordering, quality rules, null model, and multiple-testing correction are preregistered. Useful nulls preserve spatial density, source coverage, country, and year.

The current five projections produce 16 centerline-corridor hits; none passes the combined coordinate-and-bearing uncertainty gate.

Image rights

Local image registration never uploads the selected file. Public KMZ overlays are included only when the asset record explicitly documents publication authorization, attribution, and four registered corners.
